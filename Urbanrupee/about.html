About Urbanrupee

At Urbanrupee, we believe that payments are more than just transactions they are the lifeblood of every business, the bridge between ideas and growth, and the foundation for building trust with customers.

Our Vision

We are a next-generation fintech platform dedicated to transforming the way businesses accept, manage, and move money domestically. Founded with a vision to simplify and modernize payments, Urbanrupee combines advanced technology, deep financial expertise, and a customer-first approach to create solutions that are secure, scalable, and reliable.

Our Mission

Our platform empowers businesses, fintechs, marketplaces, and enterprises to focus on what matters most: growth, innovation, and delivering exceptional experiences to their customers. We make payments simple, intelligent, and accessible for businesses of all sizes.

Comprehensive Payment Solutions

We offer a comprehensive suite of solutions, including domestic payments, payment orchestration, white-label platforms, e-commerce enablement, TSP/PSP capabilities, end-to-end application development, POS machines, brand vouchers, prepaid cards, and white-label prepaid cards. Each of our offerings is designed to integrate seamlessly, providing businesses with the tools and intelligence they need to optimize operations, improve success rates, and scale effortlessly.

Our solutions are built to connect smoothly with your existing systems, helping streamline workflows and enhance overall efficiency.

They equip businesses with smart insights and tools to boost performance, increase success rates, and support scalable growth.

Groots Pay

Groots Pay supports stores and retailers who can offer Assisted Digital Financial Services—such as Aadhaar ATM, SMS payment, khata service, utility payment, prepaid cards, insurance, and money transfer—through AWESOMEPAY. Our innovative solutions are modeled to make financial transactions seamless, quick, and easy, and to empower retailer partners with tools they can trust at the counter and in the field. This clip highlights how Urbanrupee infrastructure helps deliver that experience with dependable processing and a polished brand presence at the point of sale.

Partner spotlight

Finlix Capital

We are a performance-based proprietary trading evaluation platform for traders in India: simulated challenges, clear rules, and a path to internal capital for those who demonstrate consistency and discipline.

Mission & what we do

Simulated trading: Demo-style accounts with virtual capital so you can show skill without risking your own money in the evaluation phases.

Education & evaluation: Challenges are for training, discipline, and performance assessment—not investment advice.

Not a broker: We do not execute trades for you or hold public investment funds.

POS Solutions

Urbanrupee's POS solutions empower businesses to accept payments in-store, online, or on the go, providing secure, fast, and reliable transaction processing.

Brand Vouchers

Our brand vouchers enable businesses to engage customers and drive loyalty through customizable gift and reward programs.

Prepaid Cards

With prepaid cards and white-label prepaid cards, we offer flexible solutions for employee benefits, corporate disbursements, and customer rewards.

Security, Compliance & Trust

Security, compliance, and trust are at the core of everything we do. Urbanrupee's infrastructure is built with enterprise-grade security controls, regulatory alignment, and robust risk management frameworks. Whether handling high-volume transactions, processing prepaid solutions, or managing sensitive financial data, our clients can rely on Urbanrupee to provide secure and uninterrupted payment experiences.

Our Commitment

Our mission is to make payments simple, intelligent, and accessible for businesses of all sizes. We are committed to driving innovation in the fintech space by delivering products and services that reduce complexity, streamline operations, and empower businesses to thrive in an increasingly digital economy.

At Urbanrupee, we do not just provide technology, we partner with businesses to create financial solutions that scale, innovate, and inspire. Our team of fintech experts, technologists, and operations professionals work relentlessly to ensure that every transaction, every integration, and every interaction adds value to our clients' business and their customers.

With Urbanrupee, businesses gain more than a payments platform — they gain a trusted partner dedicated to their growth, equipped with the tools, expertise, and vision to help them succeed in the modern financial landscape. We are here to enable businesses to move faster, operate smarter, and create experiences that customers love.
