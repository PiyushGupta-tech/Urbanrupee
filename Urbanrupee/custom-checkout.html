Custom Checkout

Create a fully branded, seamless checkout experience that converts visitors into customers and boosts your revenue.

What is Custom Checkout?

Urbanrupee's Custom Checkout is a white-label payment solution that allows businesses to design and control every aspect of their checkout experience. From the moment your customer clicks "Checkout" to the final confirmation screen, every element can be customized to reflect your brand identity and optimize for maximum conversion.

Unlike generic checkout pages that redirect customers to third-party sites, Custom Checkout keeps your customers on your website throughout the entire payment process, building trust and reducing cart abandonment.

Key Features

Everything you need to create the perfect checkout experience

Complete Branding

Customize colors, fonts, logos, and imagery to match your brand perfectly. Create a cohesive brand experience from start to finish.

Mobile-Optimized

Fully responsive design that looks stunning on all devices. 60%+ of your customers will checkout on mobile - we make it seamless.

Multiple Payment Methods

Support UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, Wallets, EMI options, and more - all in one integrated checkout flow.

Lightning Fast

Analytics & Insights

Track conversion rates, abandonment points, and customer behavior to continuously optimize your checkout flow.
