White Label Payment Platform

Urbanrupee's White Label Payment Platform is a comprehensive, end-to-end payment solution designed to help businesses launch their own branded payment ecosystem quickly, securely, and at scale. Built for fintech startups, enterprises, platforms, and institutions.

End-to-End Payment Solution

The solution eliminates the need to develop complex payment infrastructure while giving complete control over branding, customer experience, and business workflows. With Urbanrupee, businesses can offer payment acceptance, collections, and payouts under their own brand, powered by a robust backend that manages the entire payment lifecycle.

Comprehensive Platform Features

The platform supports seamless onboarding, transaction processing, settlement management, refunds, reconciliation, and reporting enabling businesses to operate as a full-fledged payment provider without the technical and regulatory burden. Ready-to-deploy payment infrastructure supports multiple payment methods and channels with custom workflows and operational rules.

Security and compliance are deeply embedded into the Urbanrupee white label platform. Designed for scalability and long-term growth, the platform leverages a modular and API-driven architecture that allows businesses to extend functionality as they evolve.
