Payment Orchestration

Central control over all your payment providers

Urbanrupee's payment orchestration engine gives businesses the ability to manage multiple payment providers through a single interface. Our intelligent routing logic ensures every transaction is optimized for speed, cost, and success.

  • Centralized control for all gateways and methods
  • Intelligent routing based on real-time conditions
  • Automatic failover and retry to reduce transaction loss
  • Unified dashboard with real-time monitoring
  • Add or switch providers without disruption
  • Custom rules for region, currency, and risk

Whether you are scaling to new markets or optimizing cost per transaction, Urbanrupee provides the flexibility and control needed to orchestrate payments for consistent success.
